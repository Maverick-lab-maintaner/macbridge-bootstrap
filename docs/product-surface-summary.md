# Product Surface Summary

MacBridge needs the public site and first dashboard to answer four questions with no ambiguity:

- what am I buying
- is the Mac included
- is AI included
- what do I do after signup

The current repo direction is:

- `CLI / Tooling` at `$19/month`
- `Build Pass` at `$29/day`
- `Dedicated` at `$139/month`

And the default AI model is:

- AI-ready environment included
- AI token usage not included by default
- customer brings their own provider keys unless a plan says otherwise

The detailed decision doc is in [WEBSITE_DASHBOARD_SPEC.md](./WEBSITE_DASHBOARD_SPEC.md).
